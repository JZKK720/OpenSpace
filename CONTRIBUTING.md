# Contributing

Thank you for contributing.

## Code Contributions

Unless explicitly agreed otherwise in writing, contributions to this repository are accepted under the MIT license in [LICENSE](LICENSE).

By submitting code, documentation, tests, configuration, or other non-brand content, you agree that your contribution may be used, modified, and redistributed under that MIT license.

## Branding and Trademark Boundary

Contributing to this repository does not grant any right to use:

- the `Cubecloud` name
- the `cubecloud.io` domain
- Cubecloud logos, icons, wordmarks, badges, or trade dress
- other Cubecloud-branded assets identified in [BRAND_ASSETS.md](BRAND_ASSETS.md)

Those branding rights remain reserved under [TRADEMARKS.md](TRADEMARKS.md).

## Forks and Public Redistribution

If you publish a fork publicly, you should:

- preserve upstream attribution and license notices
- rename the public forked product unless you have written permission to use Cubecloud branding
- replace Cubecloud-specific logos and brand identifiers in user-facing surfaces unless you have written permission to keep them
- avoid presenting your fork as the official Cubecloud build

## Pull Requests

Please keep pull requests technically scoped and clearly describe:

- what changed
- why it changed
- how it was validated

If a change touches branding, logos, naming, or public identity, treat it as a maintainer-reviewed branding change rather than a normal code-only contribution.
