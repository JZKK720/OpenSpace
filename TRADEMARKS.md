- the name `Cubecloud`

- product branding, service names, and brand slogans specific to Cubecloud

See [BRAND_ASSETS.md](BRAND_ASSETS.md) for the repository's explicit brand-asset notice.

All goodwill arising from use of those marks remains with the Cubecloud brand owner.

## What Others May Do

Others may, under MIT:

- use, fork, modify, and distribute the code in this repository
- submit pull requests and other code contributions
- build their own products and services from the codebase
- describe this repository factually, including nominative references such as "forked from OpenSpace" or "based on Cubecloud's fork" where legally permitted

## What Others May Not Do Without Written Permission

Others may not, without prior written permission:

- use `Cubecloud` or `cubecloud.io` as the name of their fork, product, company, package, hosted service, or deployment
- use Cubecloud logos, icons, wordmarks, or branded screenshots in a way that suggests source, sponsorship, certification, or endorsement
- present a modified build as the official Cubecloud product
- register confusingly similar names, domains, package names, or social identities tied to Cubecloud branding
- remove attribution notices and then market the result as Cubecloud

## Contribution Rule

Unless explicitly agreed otherwise in writing, contributions to this repository are accepted under the MIT license for code, documentation, and non-brand content.

Contributing does not grant any trademark or branding rights in Cubecloud, `cubecloud.io`, or related marks.

See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for the repository's contribution rule.

## Forking Guidance

If you fork this repository and redistribute a modified version, you should:

- keep the upstream and repository attribution notices intact
- rename the forked product if you are publishing it publicly
- replace Cubecloud-specific logos and brand identifiers in user-facing surfaces unless you have written permission to keep them
- make it clear that your version is an independent fork and not the official Cubecloud build

## Brand Assets

Brand assets such as logos and other Cubecloud-specific identity materials may be used only for:

- referring to the official Cubecloud project
- contributing to this repository
- internal evaluation of this repository

Any broader commercial, public, or product-branding use requires written permission.
